What is the Blindcraft and how did it start?

The Blindcraft server is a Survival SMP (survival multiplayer) server that started 3 years ago as LogicProGamings first survival map. It was uploaded to realms 2 months later after his other blind friends and some subscribers wanted to play. The server then transferred to its current home in January 2021. The Minecraft version is 1.16.5.

How many blind people play video games in the world?

The International Game Developers Association estimates 10 to 20% of people don't play video games because of a disability.
